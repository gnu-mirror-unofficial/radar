RADAR

The GNU Realtime Action-Oriented Deterministic Automaton for Remediation

Effective Network Management for the masses. We hope it to be comparible to large commercial ventures, such as HP Openview.

Come see the project plan. Also consider taking a look at Over-Cr, a similar project taking a very different approach.

Need more information? Try our suggested reading list.

We are proud to be considered part of the GNU project.

Please join our mailing lists! There are 2 of them:

  • help-radar@gnu.org - General Users discussion and support.
  • bug-radar@gnu.org - Development Discussion and bug reports.
As an example, to subscribe to help-radar, email to help-radar-request@gnu.org with the word subscribe in the body of the message.

The following projects are separate but tied in:

  • GNU FreeODBC Driver
  • SNMPTrap Library (Looking at CMU's stuff)
